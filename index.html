Project Experience

Leading transformational housing developments

Sacred Waters Development

Four-Tower Development, Langley BC
Lead development management for an Indigenous-owned, 1,600-unit, mixed-tenure community at Lot 27 – 86th Avenue in Langley. Coordinated rezoning, consultant teams, and public funding through BC Builds program; delivered a transit-oriented masterplan with 40% family-oriented housing and Indigenous-led design and amenities.
FRAFCA Residential Development, Surrey BC
Development consultant for Fraser Region Aboriginal Friendship Centre Association, overseeing offsite/onsite infrastructure and culturally grounded design. Coordinated Community Housing Fund (CHF) and Indigenous Housing Fund (IHF) applications and compliance. Project supports deeply affordable Indigenous housing in a high-need urban neighbourhood.

Arpeg Group

Fairfield SRO Redevelopment
Supported redevelopment of historic SRO into revitalized affordable housing. Assisted with design, CMHC compliance, financial reporting, and coordinated with CMHC on MLI Select program eligibility.
Comox Redevelopment
Managed redevelopment project including comprehensive tenant relocation planning to ensure minimal disruption during construction. Coordinated relocation logistics, communication strategies, and support services for existing residents.
Douglas and Fort Redevelopment
Led redevelopment planning and tenant relocation coordination for mixed-use affordable housing project. Developed and implemented tenant relocation plan with focus on maintaining community connections and minimizing displacement impacts.
And-Co, Vancouver BC
Supported pre-development and financing of BC's first WELL Platinum-certified commercial building. Managed investor pro formas and municipal approvals.

Doxa Development

Multi-Family Housing Developments
Assisted in feasibility analysis and municipal coordination for single family houses, duplexes and townhouses. Provided research, pro forma analysis, and support for land acquisition and entitlement strategies.
