About Me

I am a PMP-certified real estate development professional based in Vancouver, BC, with 5+ years of experience advancing Indigenous- and non-profit-led housing across the Lower Mainland and Vancouver Island. I lead projects from feasibility through pre-construction, blending financial rigour with community-first planning. Passionate about equitable housing solutions, I bring proven success in complex stakeholder engagement, government funding, and cross-sector collaboration.
